About Me

Some people take a straight path to a career in development; I stopped for a few coffee breaks on the way. From working in kitchens to pay for college, to selling tickets for Major League Soccer matches, to managing a branch of a rental car company I've certainly had my share of varied career experience. But it was my passion to create something lasting, and a desire to be at the forefront of innovation that led me to the Case Western Coding Boot Camp, and the challenge of mastering the skills to be a developer.

The drive to create is mostly fueled by passions. My personal projects will generally reflect those passions; Film, video games, the art of storytelling and immersive experiences, and ways to generally make ourselves happier in our day to day lives. But another of my passions is the satisfaction of overcoming challenges.

That is why this page exists; You can see what I've created, and present me with the challenge of whatever you as a person or as a company, have before you. So if you have something you need made, or made better, and would like to contact me about working on it with you, then I look forward to hearing from you.
